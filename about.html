About the Lab

Bridging the gap between theory and hardware through hands-on electronics engineering.

Welcome to RTech Labs. This platform was created as a hub for makers, students, and engineers who want to dive deep into the world of circuitry and code.

Our Mission

At RTech Labs, we believe that complex technology should be accessible. We strip away the jargon to provide clear, high-quality documentation for modern engineering projects.

What We Explore

  • Embedded Systems: Mastering microcontrollers like ESP32, Arduino, and STM32.
  • Hardware Design: Teaching the process of schematic design and professional PCB layout.
  • IoT & Robotics: Building connected systems that interact with the real world.
